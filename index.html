This quiz gives a series of questions with a countdown timer.

When your timer reaches 0, or if you answer all questions avaialble, the quiz will end. Your score will be based on the number of correct answers you were able to give, multiplied by the amount of time you had left on the clock. If you run out of time, the score you receive will be 0!
